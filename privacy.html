Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

IguanAI LLC ("IguanAI," "we," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you interact with our website and how we handle that information. It applies only to iguanai.us. If you are located outside the United States, please do not submit information through this website — we serve clients based in the United States.

What we collect

The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily provide through our contact form: your name, your company name (optional), your email address, and the contents of the message you send. We do not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising pixels, or any other form of visitor tracking on this site.

How we use it

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your inquiry and, if we begin a working relationship, to communicate with you about that engagement. We do not send marketing emails, sell or rent your data, or share it with third parties except as described below.

Service providers

Contact form submissions are processed through Google Workspace (Google Apps Script and related services), which acts as our data processor for form handling and email delivery. Google may process your submission in accordance with its own privacy terms. We do not use any other third-party processors in connection with this website.

Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain a record of our correspondence. If an inquiry does not lead to an engagement, we typically delete the associated record within twelve months.

Your rights

You may request that we access, correct, or delete the information you have submitted by emailing info@iguanai.us. We will respond to reasonable requests within thirty days.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. You share information with us at your own discretion.

Children

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be flagged on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to info@iguanai.us. IguanAI LLC is based in Delray Beach, Florida.
